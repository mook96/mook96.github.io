About muktar

Hello, my name is Muktar U, and I'm a Maryland-based front-end web developer. When I'm not working on a project for a client, I'm actively improving my JavaScript, jQuery, and Swift capabilities, in addition to JavaScript and CSS frameworks.

 

I have an ever expanding set of skills, with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery being the strongest points, and I have a lot of room for improvement to make with Swift, Python, and AngularJS. I get most of my design inspiration from observing product design, packaging, and industrial architecture.

 

My secret weapons for front-end web development include: projects on gitHub, resource pages of Udemy instructors, and the discussion boards on StackOverflow.
